EO4Des

Earth Observation for Desertification

“When resources are degraded, we start competing for them. [...] So one way to promote peace is to promote sustainable management and equitable distribution of resources.” (Wangari Maathai). This concept is at the base of the Earth Observation for Desertification (EO4Des) project, aimed at studying the desertification process in Puglia and Sicilia contexts by applying the most advanced Earth Observations (EO) data.
